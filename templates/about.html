our story

GreenSpace Farms started in a sloppy community
in Ogun state, Nigeria, with one strategic goal

“To guarantee a daily and consistent  supply of 
fresh organic food on your tables and kitchen daily ”

Since start-up, our operations and
expansion have been driven by our
core value of practicing Sustainable
Farming, this has been the pillars of
our growth strategy.

As good custodian of the Land and God’s given natural resources,
we are also engaging the local community to be part of our growth
story by providing them with employment and professional advisory services for local subsistence farmers.

We also engage young Agricpreneurs
to drive our businesses and empower
them to think outside the box.

To this end all our produce is gathered by hand, and we process
them on the same day to ensure they are fresh and of top-quality, as we deliver to the various retail outlets who have given us the
opportunity to serve them.
